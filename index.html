What I Do

We live now, a true technological revolution called World Wide Web. I am proud and eager to be in the first line, contributing with ideas, knowledge and enthusiasm to develop the future.

Even if 20 years have passed since my first line of code, my interest and motivation is still the same. I can say I am the few ones for whom the job and my hobbies are the same thing.

Full Stack Development

Four years ago I switch from LAMP Stack to MEAN Stack and never looked back ever since. Proud of that decision everyday, as Javascript and Co, proved to be the right technology to follow and evelove.

Internet of Things

Since practically anything can be connected to the net, application of IoT depends upon the utility of the connected-ness, and practicability of handling the information generated and transmitted by smart objects.

Artificial Intelligence

If the other 2 of my interests represent the past and the present, Artificial Intelligence is definitely the future for me. Along with good developing skills and connectivity of IOT objects, AI will be the technology of 2020 and beyond.
